FlightGear scenery is available for the entire world

The base FlightGear download starts you off with a small subset of scenery around KSFO (San Francisco, CA USA). If you want to fly to or from other airports, you will need to obtain additional scenery via the following three techniques.

TerraSync

Available as part of FlightGear is a program called TerraSync. This utility runs in the background as a separate process, monitors your position, and either downloads or updates the latest greatest scenery from the master scenery server just in time.

FlightGear DVD

If you have a slow/expensive net connection, prefer physical media, or want to help the FlightGear project, please consider FlightGear on DVD. Along with the program DVD, you can get the entire world scenery collection on a 3-DVD set.
