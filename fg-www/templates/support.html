Internet Relay Chat

For live discussions and help there is the FlightGear IRC server. If your new to IRC there's the short FlightGear IRC tutorial.

  • The main site and channel is irc.flightgear.org#flightgear
  • The backup channel in the event of the main server beind down is at freenode irc.freenode.net#flightgear

Mailing List Archives

On Dec 20, 2005 we moved our mailing lists from the FlightGear.org server to SourceForge.net. This means our archives are divided into two locations, the pre-switchover FlightGear archives, and the post-switchover SourceForge archives.
