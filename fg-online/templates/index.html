With multi player, the FlightGear simulator take's on a few more dimensions of simulated reality, you can for example:-

  • Chat to other pilots and ATC
  • Fly in tandem - difficult and not as easy as you think
  • Participate in ATC
  • Anything you want to do as a group

The way it works is than you as a pilot with your FlightGear Aircraft instance connects to an online MultiPlayer server. The Multiplayer servers in turn, chat to each each other and relay your flight position, along with thousands of others as a "cluster", many times a second.
